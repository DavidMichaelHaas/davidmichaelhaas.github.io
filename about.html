ABOUT

Hi, I'm David Haas (he/him)! Currently based in Darmstadt, Germany, I am passionate about Portrait, Street, and Music Photography. These different genres have one thing in common - capturing human emotion in the moment. Whether it is during the rehearsal of an orchestra, or somewhere in the world on a busy street, capturing small moments and sharing them with others fascinates me in my photography. I like to shoot a mixture of colour and black and white.

I am currently in the process of pursuing a second degree in psychology, in addition to my bachelor's in computer science. Besides photography, I enjoy reading, making, and listening to music, cooking, and playing video games in my free time!

Feel free to contact me via my email address or my socials, cheers!
